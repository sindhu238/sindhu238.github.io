This privacy policy describes how we use any information that you give Go London when you use the application. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

All the personal information you share with our app will be in accordance with this privacy statement.  I will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our app, it may require you to provide us with your geolocation information (using GPS).

The app does use GPS that may collect information used to identify your location which will not be stored with us or sent to any other services. It is just used to personalize the nearby stops around your location.

Security

I value your trust in providing us your personal information and it is secure. We do not collect it or share it with anyone else.


Controlling your personal information

You can also turn off the collection or use of your personal geolocation information by disabling geolocation reporting (GPS) services on your device and use the search option to get nearby stops near that location.


Android Permissions


Android 6.0 introduced a new permission model, that allows apps to request permissions at run-time. Below are the permissions that this app uses:


LOCATION: This permission is needed to obtain your location when finding nearby stations using GPS.


INTERNET: This permission is used to get live bus and tube arrivals provided by TFL service


NETWORK_STATE: This permission is used to check if valid network is connected before making requests to TFL service


ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: This permission is used to get your precise location.
